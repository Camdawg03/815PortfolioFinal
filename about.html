More About Me

Hello! I’m Camdyn and I am a UX researcher-in-training who strives to create inclusive, intuitive, and impactful digital experiences . I graduated with my bachelors degree in Information Sciences in 2024 an will graduate with my masters in User Experience in Spring 2026.

My work always puts the user in the driver’s seat; digging into user needs and creating meaningful and impactful design solutions. I have built a solid foundation in user research, assessable design, HTML coding, and usability testing. From wireframes to team projects, I am always focus on unraveling and creating the best experiences for every user. I am very excited to keep learning, growing, and designing as I step into the big, wide world of UX.
